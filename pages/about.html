Career Objective

Recent graduate from the University of Sydney studying Interaction Design with experience in front-end development, I aim to bridge the gap between technology and empathetic design. I’m highly interested in the similarities and differences of problem solving in both UX design and IT development, finding contemporary ways to unite these two disciplines.
Currently I’m studying at Coder Academy to further my interest in full-stack development. Throughout my studies, I’ve gained valuable experience in professional design and development practices, particularly in:

  • Qualitative and Quantitative Research
  • User Experience/Interaction Design
  • Front-end Development - HTML, CSS, Javascript

Hobbies

Outside of design and development, you can find me taking digital portraits of with my FujiFilm X-T2 and occasionally with my Canon film camera. I love photography! I’m also a blue belt in Brazilian Jiu Jitsu, training for more than 2 years now, teaching kids martial arts on the side.
